About the Owner



Sarah Martinez was born and raised in New Mexico. She studied at Anderson School of Management at the University of New Mexico.

When she was young, her mom knew she could always find Sarah with one of the many animals she had as a child. Sarah moved out and got her own place she decided to get a puppy. She quickly realized that she would need to either take this puppy to the groomer every 6 weeks or learn how to do it herself. She found her love for grooming by practing on her own 4 Yorkshire Terriers.
